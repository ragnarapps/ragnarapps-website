Features

Everything You Need for Air Diving

Built for air diving supervisors operating in compliance with US Navy and NORSOK standards.

Embedded Deco Tables

US Navy Rev 7 and NORSOK tables built in — no paper tables needed.

One-Plus-One Rule

Automatically applied — always rounds to the next safer depth and time.

Live Auto-Update

Deco schedule updates automatically as the diver's bottom time increases.

SurDO2 Profile Chart

Visual chamber decompression profile showing O2 periods and air breaks.

Task Log

Voice and manual task logging with automatic timestamps.

PDF Export

Professional dive record PDF generated automatically on submission.
