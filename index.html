About Ragnar Apps

Bringing Diving Into
the Digital Age

For decades, offshore diving operations have relied on carbon paper, handwritten logs, and manual scanning to document critical dive data. This platform is trying to streamline the data flow from supervisor to admin and client.

Our platform is purpose-built for the offshore subsea industry — designed by people who understand the industry, and the operational demands of life at sea. Every feature exists because it solves a real problem on real vessels.

With Ragnar Apps, dive supervisors complete digital records in real time. HR and management receive instant notifications. Reports are generated automatically and analyses are created to measure production.

We are committed to streamlining the processes between diving companies and their clients — making operations more transparent, more efficient, and fully traceable from any device, anywhere in the world.
